About Zoomeze

Private dog play sessions with indoor and outdoor fun.

Zoomeze Dog Play Zone is designed as a private play experience, giving dogs space to enjoy the facilities without sharing the slot with other groups. The site includes an indoor soft play area and an outdoor play zone, making it a fun option in all weather.

  • Private booking slots for one group at a time
  • Indoor soft play and outdoor play area
  • Weekday evening and weekend availability
  • Ideal for energetic dogs, nervous dogs and special occasions
