Private Hire Facility

Zoomeze Dog Play Zone operates as a private hire indoor and outdoor dog play facility.

You will receive a key code for the front door lock within your booking confirmation email.

CCTV operates at all entry points and communal areas.

Session Duration

Each booking provides 25 minutes of active play time within a 30 minute booking slot.

Please arrive at your allocated time and leave after 25 minutes to allow safe changeover.

Booking two consecutive sessions provides approximately 55 minutes of play time.

Parking

On-site parking is provided and clearly marked with signs stating “WALKEZE / ZOOMEZE – Park Here”.

Booking & Payment

All bookings are non‑refundable including missed sessions.

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the booking to be considered for rescheduling.

Number of Dogs

Each booking includes up to 2 dogs. Maximum 10 dogs per booking.

Cleaning & Waste

Cleaning equipment including mop and bucket, commercial dog waste bin outside the front door, clean water bowls and fresh drinking water are provided.

Liability

Use of the facility and equipment is entirely at the owner's risk.
