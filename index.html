About Zoomeze

A private play experience for dogs in Stockport.

Zoomeze Dog Play Zone is designed for dogs to enjoy the facilities without sharing the slot with another group. The site includes an indoor soft play area and an outdoor play zone, making it a fun option in all weather.

It is ideal for energetic dogs, friendship groups, nervous dogs and special occasions. Bookings are confirmed at the time of payment, and once a slot is booked it becomes unavailable automatically.
